The Speech

In this part many people get very nervous but don't worry I have more tips. -No reading this is the biggest mistake anyone can make. -Memorise your speech and don't read it make it like a conversation. -Use props . -Trasnitions. -Have a good pace while reading (This helps a lot because then only the audience understands what your speaking.) -Dress in a good manner (My reccomednation:a coat and good jeans ). -Your room ( this matters a lot )(A good background a clean room and a neat table). -Have a badge this very imrpotant it makes an impact.
